Japan Travel Guide


Travel To Japan, With A Plan


It is recommended that for the best experience in Japan is to travel with someone who knows moderate Japanese. The best way to travel from place to place would be by the Shinkansen (bullet train). Depending on where you are traveling and how long you are staying for check if it is worth getting the weekly or monthly pass. Most people prefer to travel to Japan around April to May or September to November, since the temperatures aren’t blazing around those times, and it’s also blossoming seasons, where the Sakura trees are most visible. While you are in Japan, if you have a few hundres lying around, you should throw your money at "Kobe Beef": extremely expensive steak, which prices can vary depending on the grade you get in it (A1-A5, A5 being the most expensive up at $150 for 100 grams). Although this steak can come at a fortune, is consider cheap compare to US, since Wagyu(breed of cows) are mainly bred in Japan.

YOU DON’T TIP IN JAPAN. TIPPING IN JAPAN MAY SEEM TO COME OFF AS BEING DISRESPECTFUL.
